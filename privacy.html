Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This site is built on one principle: we want to tell you something interesting, not collect data about you.

What this site collects

Nothing that identifies you.

We do not log your IP address, location, device, or browser. We do not use cookies of our own. We do not run analytics software. We do not have accounts, forms, or any mechanism to collect personal information. The only metric we are interested in is aggregate traffic volume — total visits, no breakdown by user.

Third-party advertising

This site displays ads to cover some costs. Those ads are served by third parties who have their own data practices.

Google AdSense

If Google AdSense ads are shown on this page, Google may:

  • Set cookies in your browser to serve ads based on your prior browsing history
  • Use the DoubleClick cookie to measure ad performance
  • Collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google's Ad Settings or the NAI opt-out tool.

EthicalAds

If EthicalAds ads are shown, they do not use cookies, do not track users across sites, and target ads by page topic only — not by your personal profile. See EthicalAds' privacy policy for details.

Hosting infrastructure

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. All traffic is served over HTTPS.

As part of normal web hosting operations, GitHub may process request metadata such as IP addresses and user-agent strings. We do not have access to these logs and do not process them ourselves.

See GitHub's Privacy Statement for full details of their data practices.

Cookies

We set no cookies ourselves. Google AdSense may set cookies if those ads are active. You can block or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings without affecting your use of this site.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights including:

  • Access — ask what data is held about you
  • Deletion — ask for data to be erased
  • Opt-out — opt out of personalised advertising (see Google Ad Settings above)

Because we hold no personal data ourselves, any such requests relating to ad data should be directed to Google or the relevant ad provider.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If our data practices change materially, we will update the date at the top of this page. Given the minimal nature of this site, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

Disclaimer

This policy covers isai.money only. It does not cover any third-party sites linked from this page.
